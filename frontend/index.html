🧠 How It Works ▸
1
Frame Capture

Video frames are extracted at regular intervals or captured live from your webcam at 640×480.

2
Keypoint Detection

MediaPipe detects 67 body + hand joints per frame (25 body, 21 per hand) with x, y coordinates and confidence.

3
Normalization

Keypoints are shoulder-anchored and normalized to a consistent signing space. Missing joints are interpolated.

4
AI Translation

A T5 transformer model with temporal convolution encodes the keypoint sequence and decodes it to English text.
